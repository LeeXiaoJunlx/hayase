About Me

Hi! I'm Hayase Yuuka, a frontend developer with a deep passion for anime and Japanese culture. My journey in web development started when I wanted to create my own anime fan site, and since then I've fallen in love with the process of turning ideas into interactive experiences.

I specialize in creating visually stunning and highly functional web applications with modern technologies like React, Vue.js, and Tailwind CSS. My anime-inspired design aesthetic helps me create unique user interfaces that stand out from the crowd.
